Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

1. Introduction

SnapAPI (“we”, “us”, or “our”) provides an AI-ready web scraping API (URL to structured text). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website and services. By using SnapAPI, you agree to this policy.

2. What we do not do with your content

We do not store customer page content for our own purposes. URLs you submit are loaded only to extract readable content in real time. We do not use your scraped text for advertising, profiling, model training, or persistent analytics beyond short operational logs.

3. API responses

Scrape results are returned directly in the HTTP response (JSON). We do not require object storage for core delivery; any optional archival you perform on your side is your responsibility.

4. Account and operational data

To operate the service (authentication, API keys, billing tiers, quotas, and abuse prevention), we may process limited account and technical data such as email addresses, API identifiers, usage counts, request metadata (e.g. timestamps, endpoints, target URLs as needed for logs), and server logs. This processing is for service delivery, security, and compliance, not for building advertising profiles from the content of pages you scrape.

5. Cookies and sessions

Our dashboard and login flows may use cookies or similar technologies (e.g. session cookies) to maintain your session. These are used for authentication and security, not for cross-site tracking or behavioral advertising.
